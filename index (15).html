Procedures & Policy
Standard Operating Procedures
SOP-01 · VEHICLE USAGE
All company vehicles must be logged before and after every shift. A Delivery Log or Field Report must be submitted each session. Unauthorised vehicle use is prohibited.
SOP-02 · SHIFT SYSTEM
All employees must clock in via the Shift System. Shifts without a corresponding log may not count toward quotas. Break times must be accurately recorded.
SOP-03 · COMMUNICATION
Professional radio etiquette at all times. Client communications must be logged. Do not discuss company info in public channels.
SOP-04 · INCIDENT REPORTING
Any on-road incident, vehicle damage, or interaction with emergency services must be reported within the same session. Failure to report is a disciplinary offence.
SOP-05 · METAGAMING POLICY
Information from portal logs must never be used in-game unless your character was present. Any breach results in immediate suspension.
SOP-06 · CONDUCT
Professional conduct is expected at all times. Uniform must be worn while on duty.
